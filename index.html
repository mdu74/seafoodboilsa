Our Story

Seafood Boil SA was established in the year 2020 in Kyalami North of Johannesburg South Africa. After a 2 year extensive search to find a seafood boil restaurant in South Africa- our luck ran dry, so we decided to put together the tried and tested recipe we have been conjuring for over 2 year since the the viral seafood boil Mukbangs sensation launched.

We literally had fear of missing out so we hopped on the wagon and discovered our own recipe to our famous ‘party in your mouth’ •Cajun Butter Sauce• which is all now all the rave in South Africa.

Origin of Seafood boils

Seafood boils originated in Georgia and South Carolina in 1985. Although really popular in South Carolina in The United States of America, through restaurant establishments. Seafood boils are now the hot, juicy and trending topic around the world and has given You Tube content creators a different platform from which to gain subscribers.
